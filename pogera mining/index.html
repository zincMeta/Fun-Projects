About Us

The Porgera Gold Mine employs over 3,300 Papua New Guineans and over the life of mine it has produced more than 20 million ounces of gold and contributed approximately 10% of Papua New Guinea’s total annual exports. The Porgera Joint Venture (PJV) owns the Porgera Mine which is operated by Barrick (Niugini) Limited ("BNL") on behalf of the JV partners.
